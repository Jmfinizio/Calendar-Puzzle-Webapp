Author: Johnathan Finizio

Calendar Puzzle

How to play:

  • Drag and drop the pieces onto the board
  • Click on a piece in the playing area to remove it
  • To rotate the piece, click on it in the work area and use the arrow keys
  • To flip the piece, click on it in the work area and use the space bar
  • Pieces cannot be placed such that they will go out of bounds, or cover the date
